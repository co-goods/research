# network coordination

> **noun.** The structural condition by which distributed participants in a network align their actions toward shared outcomes without central direction.
>
> **verb (intransitive).** To engage in network-coordination — to act in ways that align with other distributed participants without explicit central direction. *Present: coordinating; past: coordinated.*
