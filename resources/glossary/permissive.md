# permissive

> **adjective.** (of a license) Granting broad rights to use, modify, and redistribute a work — including in closed or commercial works — with minimal conditions, typically only attribution. MIT and CC BY are permissive licenses; contrast [[resources/glossary/copyleft|copyleft]].
